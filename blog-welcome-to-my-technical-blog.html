Starting My Journey at UC San Diego

I'm excited to begin sharing my experiences and insights as I start my Master's program at UC San Diego, specializing in Electronic Circuits and Systems.

My Background

Coming from IIT Hyderabad with a strong foundation in analog and RF IC design, I've had the privilege of working on several exciting projects:

  • RFID tag design and successful tapeout in 180nm CMOS at WiLO Networks
  • Advanced FinFET node design (<5nm) at Intel Corporation
  • Power management silicon characterization at Texas Instruments

What to Expect from This Blog

I'll be sharing content on:

  1. Deep dives into analog IC design techniques - from basic building blocks to advanced architectures
  2. Insights from coursework and research - what I'm learning at UCSD
  3. Industry experiences and lessons learned - practical tips from my internships and work experience
  4. Project updates - documenting my design journey and challenges

Let's Connect!

Feel free to reach out if you want to discuss IC design, have questions about my work, or just want to connect. You can find my contact information on the home page.

Stay tuned for more technical content, project updates, and reflections on the exciting world of analog and mixed-signal IC design!
